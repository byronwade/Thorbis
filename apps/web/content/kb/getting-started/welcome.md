# Welcome to Thorbis

Welcome to **Thorbis**, the modern business management platform designed specifically for service companies. Whether you're managing HVAC, plumbing, electrical, or any other service-based business, Thorbis provides all the tools you need to run your operations efficiently.

## What is Thorbis?

Thorbis is a comprehensive business management platform that helps you:

- **Manage Customers** - Keep track of all your customer information, contact details, and service history
- **Schedule Jobs** - Organize and schedule service calls with your team
- **Track Invoices** - Create, send, and track invoices and payments
- **Manage Equipment** - Track equipment installations and maintenance schedules
- **Handle Inventory** - Keep track of materials and supplies
- **Process Payments** - Accept payments online and track payment methods

## Getting Started

To get started with Thorbis, follow these simple steps:

1. **Create Your Account** - Sign up for a free account at [thorbis.com](https://thorbis.com)
2. **Set Up Your Company** - Add your company information and customize your settings
3. **Add Your Team** - Invite team members and set up roles and permissions
4. **Import Your Data** - Import existing customers, jobs, and other data
5. **Start Using Thorbis** - Begin managing your business operations
